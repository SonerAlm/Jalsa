Frequently asked questions

What is an eSIM and how does it work?
An eSIM is a digital SIM built into your phone. Instead of inserting a plastic card, you install your plan with a single QR scan and switch it on in settings — your existing number and SIM can stay put.
Is my phone compatible with eSIM?
Most phones released from around 2019 onward support eSIM, including iPhone XR and later, Samsung Galaxy S21 and later, and Google Pixel 3 and later. Your phone also needs to be carrier-unlocked.
How quickly can I activate after purchasing?
Installation takes a couple of minutes. You can set up your eSIM before you travel and choose exactly when to activate, so your plan's validity starts when you arrive at Jalsa.
Will my eSIM work if I'm travelling from another country?
Yes. As long as your device is unlocked and eSIM-capable, you can buy and install before you fly. The plan connects to the UK network the moment you land — no physical SIM swap required.
What if I need help during the event?
Support is available in the Amigo app throughout Jalsa, with multilingual help on hand. You can reach the team any time at help@amigosim.com.
Can I top up my data if I run out?
Absolutely. Top up in seconds from the Amigo app without losing connection — handy if family arrivals or live streams use more data than expected.
