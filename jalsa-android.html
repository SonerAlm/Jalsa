Good to know

FAQ

What is an eSIM and how does it work?
An eSIM is a digital SIM built into your phone. Instead of a plastic card, you install your plan with one tap and switch it on in settings.
Is my phone compatible with eSIM?
Most phones from around 2019 onward support eSIM, including iPhone XR and later. Your phone also needs to be carrier-unlocked.
How quickly can I activate after purchasing?
Installation takes a couple of minutes. Set up before you travel and choose exactly when to activate.
Will my eSIM work travelling from another country?
Yes. If your device is unlocked and eSIM-capable, buy and install before you fly — it connects to the UK network when you land.
What if I need help during the event?
Support is in the app throughout Jalsa, with multilingual help on hand. Reach us at help@amigosim.com.
Can I top up my data if I run out?
Yes — top up in seconds from the app without losing connection.
